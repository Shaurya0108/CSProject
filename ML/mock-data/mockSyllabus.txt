Course Description: This course covers the design and analysis of algorithms and data structures. Topics include asymptotic analysis, recurrences, and algorithm design techniques such as divide-and-conquer, greedy method, and dynamic programming. Additional topics include graph algorithms, sorting and searching, and NP-completeness.
